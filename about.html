About Weather Inc

Weather Inc is a leading provider of accurate and reliable weather forecasts, with a mission to help people make informed decisions based on the weather. Our team of experienced meteorologists use the latest technology and data analysis techniques to deliver high-quality weather information to our customers.

Our History

Weather Inc was founded in 2005 by a group of meteorologists who wanted to create a better way to deliver weather information to the public. They saw a need for more accurate and detailed forecasts, especially for severe weather events, and wanted to provide a service that could help people stay safe and make informed decisions.

Our Team

Our team of meteorologists includes some of the most experienced and knowledgeable professionals in the field. They have years of experience in forecasting a wide range of weather conditions, from severe storms and hurricanes to daily temperature and precipitation patterns. We also have a dedicated team of developers, data analysts, and customer support staff who work together to deliver the best possible service to our customers.

Our Services

At Weather Inc, we offer a range of services to meet the needs of our customers. These include:

  • Detailed weather forecasts for locations around the world
  • Severe weather alerts and notifications
  • Customized weather data and reports for businesses and organizations
  • Weather data APIs for developers
  • 24/7 customer support and expert guidance
